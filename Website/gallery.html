Welcome to my humble portfolio.

Here you’ll find my artwork across all stages of production. I’m an instinctual sculptor, though my curiosity pulls me in many directions. Fifteen years of practice and study have brought me here, and I’m still learning.

I love creatures, and critters, and magical curiosities. Forms that are innate yet mysterious in the human mind. If I can achieve the path between sophistication and the crude psyche of living things, I will be content.

If you’d like to see my work in person, visit Terra Studios. Ask the staff to point you to my booth, and feel free to browse or sign the guest book. If I’m in the studio, come say hello. :^)
