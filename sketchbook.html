My Usual Process

Step 1 — Concept & Inspiration

Briefly explain the idea, references, and the mood you’re aiming for. What sparked it? What do you want the audience to feel?

Step 2 — Sketching & Reflecting

Fast studies and turnarounds to lock pose and proportions.

Step 3 — Prototype & Editing

Test prints, support strategy, articulation, and small fixes. Adjust silhouette/read from arm’s length.

Step 4 — Final Making

Print one last time at the optimal print angle. Clean, cure, and paint the master with the intended finish.

Step 5 — Merchandising

Prepare labels, product cards, and price tags. Stage a small phone‑vertical scene for shop or web display.
